What we do

Sweet. Savory. Waffles.

“Who doesn’t love brunch?,” I realized one day while eating a waffle. After an 11-year career with the Navy, I decided to start the Waffle Wagon here in Denton. My friends and family helped to taste-test while I tried out different sandwiches and toppings until I got them just right; all served on hot, crispy waffles.

We move around a lot, so check out our Facebook and Twitter feeds for our location in Denton. One day the Waffle Wagon may be brick and mortar, but until then, come on out and visit us on the truck!
